Our novel Structure-Informed Super-Resolution technique leverages advanced deep learning architectures with structural priors to achieve unprecedented quality in scientific image enhancement. This breakthrough research addresses critical limitations in existing methods and provides superior performance across diverse imaging modalities.

Novel Architecture

Innovative structure-informed network design that incorporates domain knowledge and structural priors for enhanced feature learning and reconstruction quality.

Scientific Focus

Specifically designed for scientific imaging applications with rigorous evaluation on medical, biological, and materials science datasets.

Superior Performance

Consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods including VDSR, ESRGAN, SwinIR, and HMA-Net across all evaluation metrics.

Methodology

Structure-Aware Feature Extraction

Our method employs multi-scale structural analysis to capture both local texture details and global structural coherence, enabling more informed reconstruction decisions.

Adaptive Loss Function

A novel combination of perceptual, structural, and adversarial losses specifically tailored for scientific imaging requirements and quality assessment.
